About me

I started my career in User Experience and Interaction Design in 2016, as a junior scholarship researcher at Media Lab, a research center from Federal University of Goiás (UFG), where I got my Bachelor's degree.
I was awarded twice during the graduation, first as one of The Best Scientific Research Project (2017) for the work done at Media Lab and also as The Best Final Paper in a competition for entrepreneurship and innovation (2019), for the ideation of Behave, a digital product for clinical behavioral psychologists and their patient's management needs.
‍
From 2017-2018, I also worked as a junior Brand Designer & Art Director for cultural projects, small brands, and even for a football team - managing social media content, inbound marketing campaigns, packaging, and printed materials.

‍Since 2018, I work with startups and large companies mainly as a Product Designer. I have experience in collaborating with Sales, Marketing, Customer Success & Agile Development teams to improve solutions & work processes focusing on data-driven and customer-centric decision-making. 
‍
I gained a lot of experience collaborating with an international team of Designers, Product Managers, Business Analysts, and Developers for worldwide projects while working at Netcracker with fellow colleagues from Russia, Ukraine, and Canadá. I designed well-conceived and usable UIs for several Graphical Tools focused on Network Automation, Orchestration, Data Center Management, and Customer Engagement Platforms. 
‍
Currently, I work at Nubank, the biggest digital bank in the world. In my personal time, I create homemade music alongside colleagues and members of Parallaxis Recordings, an independent group of producers and artists from Goiânia, Brazil.
